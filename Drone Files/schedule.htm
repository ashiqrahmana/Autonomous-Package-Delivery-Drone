Grand Finale
e-Yantra Robotics Competition (eYRC 2020-21)

Finally....The Wait is Over!! We are happy to announce the National Finals of e-Yantra Robotics Competitions: eYRC 2020-21 Finale.

Owing to the current pandemic and having experienced the various online platforms during this lockdown, this event will be quite different and unique from the "normal" eYRC finale we usually have.

We will be streaming all the Themes as they happen live from March 30th to 3rd April, 2021 on our YouTube channel , so invite your friends and family to come and watch the ingenuity of you and your team members and other participating students, their grit and innovation. You have toiled for months to get to this point and their virtual presence will certainly cheer your team.
