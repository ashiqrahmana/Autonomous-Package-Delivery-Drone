ROS Melodic Morenia

ROS Melodic Morenia is the twelfth ROS distribution release. It was released on May 23rd, 2018.

Platforms

ROS Melodic Morenia is primarily targeted at the Ubuntu 18.04 (Bionic) release, though other Linux systems as well as Mac OS X, Android, and Windows are supported to varying degrees. For more information on compatibility on other platforms, please see REP 3: Target Platforms. It will also support Ubuntu 17.10 Artful and Debian Stretch.
